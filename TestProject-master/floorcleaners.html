Kampfer Floor Cleaner

KampferTM is specialized product to meet one stop solution for all floor cleaning needs. It contains concentrate liquid which adhere to surface tightly to clean it deeply.

KampferTM is such product that make floor cleaning a less tiring and left no frustration as it gives quick result with its effective, reliable, strong and disinfecting formula with pleasant odour. Floor cleaning will not be hated work then.

KampferTM shines your floor, disinfects and make it more hygienic and fresh with its regular usage.

KampferTM is better over all other bleach, acid, phenyl and ordinary floor cleaner.

By regular use of KampferTM stain built up and germ growth will reduced and your floor will remain as fresh as new.

Key Features:

  • All yellowish stain, dirt, dust removal
  • Kills 99% of germs
  • Helps fight unpleasant odour and any unpleasant smell
  • Better over other ordinary bleach, acid and phenyl
  • Neutral pH- safe on most finished floor
  • No rinse required
  • Floral fragrance
Also available in: Four fresh, clean scents: Floral, Lemon, Jasmine, Rose (200ml, 250ml, 500ml, 1,000ml, 2000ml, 5000ml)

USAGE INFORMATION:

  • Take 2 full (20 ml) caps of concentrate in 4 liter water.
  • Apply it on surface with cloth, sponge, mop.
  • Wipe it out dry.
