Kampfer Floor Cleaner

Kampfer Floor Cleaner is formulated to kil 99% germs and to remove all stains, leaving shiny surface. Its unique formula gives long lasting floral fragrance.

Ingredients: Benzalkonium Chloride, Sodium laureth sulphate, Sodium bicarbonate, Perfume

Application: Floor and Bathroom surface: Use one full cap(10 ml) in 5 litre of water. Mop the surface gently. For Kitchen: Apply undiluted on surface. Mop after 10 minutes.

Caution: Use only in Toilet bowls. Keep closed when noy in use. Keep away from reach of children. Do not swallow. If contacted with eyes wash with plenty of water. Seek medical advice. If swallowed, rinse mouth with water. Do not induce vomitting nad seek medical assistance immediately.
