Kampfer Glass Cleaner

KampferTM is specialized product to meet all glass cleaning needs. It contains liquid which adhere to glass surface to clean it deeply all over.

KampferTM gives quick result with its effective, reliable, strong and ammonia free glass cleaning formula. It has very fresh and pleasant odour to make you work happy. Glass cleaning is no more work out exercise.

KampferTM shines your utensil by removing 100 % all stains on your glass. KampferTM is better over all other glass cleaner. KampferTM will help keep your glass as shiny as new.

KampferTM bottle is designed to ease its handling which will not allow to loosen hand grip on bottle. Its pump is design to spray fine mist perfectly on your glass.

Key Features:

  • All stain removal
  • Ammonia Free
  • Pleasant smell
  • Better over other ordinary cleaners.
Also available in: Blue (200ml, 250ml, 500ml, 1,000ml, 2000ml, 5000ml)

USAGE INFORMATION:

  • Spray fine mist 6-8 inches from surface through nozzle on glass surface.
  • Wipe with clean and dry lint free cloth.
Note: Check USER MANUAL before use on electronics item. Do not use on wood, varnished surfaces, metal alloy such as brass, copper and pewter. Do not apply heat.
